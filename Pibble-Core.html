What is a pibble?

Pibble is a short name for a pitbull, they are very cute when they're first born but look quite aggressive when they grow.
The name was given to them as a way of lessening the fear of the breed because of their reputation of being violent dogs.
From what I have seen of this breed they are the biggest softies you will ever meet. Alongside the name there is also a meme known as "pibble core".
